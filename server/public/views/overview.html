What do you know about the current presidential front-runners?


There's currently a lot of hype about the unusually large pool of prospective candidates for this election cycle's presidential election.

While the pool has arguably been narrowed down to a pair of front runners from each major party, many in America are still ill-informed on the political positions held by these candidates.

Compiled on this page are a collection of biographies for the current leaders of each major party. Feel free to navigate between their pages to read about their views and compare stances on major national issues.
